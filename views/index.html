Dr. Gregory House

About the Show

House M.D. is an American medical drama that aired from 2004 to 2012. It follows the brilliant but misanthropic Dr. Gregory House, who leads a team of diagnosticians solving complex medical cases.

Awards & Recognition

  • 2 Golden Globe Awards
  • 5 Primetime Emmy Awards
  • Peabody Award in 2005
  • Multiple People's Choice Awards
